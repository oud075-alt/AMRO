# Fear Behavior

## Observable Structure
- volatility spike
- aggressive rejection
- spread expansion
- fast downward displacement
- unstable liquidity

## Visual Characteristics
- long bearish candles
- large wick rejection
- chaotic movement
- irregular continuation

## Emotional Interpretation
- crowd fear
- forced exit behavior
- panic reaction

## Common Outcome
- short-term overreaction
- violent reversal possibility
- continuation under high pressure

## Failure Mode
- fake panic
- liquidity sweep
- news distortion
