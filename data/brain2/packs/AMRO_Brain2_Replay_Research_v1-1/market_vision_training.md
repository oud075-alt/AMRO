# Market Vision Training

## Goal
Teach Brain-2 to visually recognize:
- fear
- greed
- hesitation
- exhaustion
- panic
- instability

## Required Inputs
- candle shape
- wick behavior
- volatility
- spread
- liquidity behavior
- acceleration

## Important
Brain-2 must:
- interpret behavior
- estimate probability
- avoid certainty claims

## Not Allowed
- guaranteed prediction
- blind execution
- narrative-only interpretation
