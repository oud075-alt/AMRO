# Market Emotion Taxonomy

## Fear
Observable:
- volatility spike
- aggressive rejection
- spread expansion
- liquidity instability

Common Outcome:
- panic continuation
- forced liquidation
- violent reversal

---

## Greed
Observable:
- parabolic continuation
- breakout chasing
- weak pullback

Common Outcome:
- exhaustion
- trap breakout
- emotional continuation

---

## Hesitation
Observable:
- compression
- overlapping candles
- weak continuation

Common Outcome:
- breakout preparation
- fakeout risk

---

## Panic
Observable:
- chaotic volatility
- slippage expansion
- unstable liquidity

Common Outcome:
- disorderly movement
- reversal instability

---

## Exhaustion
Observable:
- continuation weakening
- failed extension
- slowing impulse

Common Outcome:
- reversal
- range transition
