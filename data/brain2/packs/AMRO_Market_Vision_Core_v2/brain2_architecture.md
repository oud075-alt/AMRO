# Brain-2 Architecture

## Layer 1 — Vision Engine
Input:
- candle structure
- wick behavior
- volatility
- spread
- liquidity
- acceleration
- displacement

Purpose:
Convert chart movement into visual behavioral states.

---

## Layer 2 — Behavior Translation Engine
Translate:
- compression -> hesitation
- impulsive expansion -> aggression
- chaotic volatility -> panic
- rejection -> refusal pressure

Purpose:
Create market emotional context.

---

## Layer 3 — Similarity Memory Engine
Functions:
- historical retrieval
- pattern comparison
- failure recall
- regime similarity

Purpose:
Provide synthetic market memory.

---

## Layer 4 — Probability Engine
Estimate:
- continuation probability
- fakeout probability
- instability probability
- exhaustion probability

Important:
No certainty output allowed.

---

## Layer 5 — Context Compression Engine
Convert:
raw metrics -> governance context

Example:
Instead of:
- ATR
- RSI
- spread delta

Send:
- unstable continuation
- elevated fakeout risk
- emotional expansion detected
